# Archero Abilities Reference

Source: https://archero.fandom.com/wiki/Category:Abilities

Abilities (skills) are power-ups obtained after every hero level up (choice of 3). Also obtainable from angels.

Abilities reset at the end of each adventure.

---

## Arrow Abilities

### Multishot
- **Image:** ![Multishot](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/b/b2/Multishot.png/revision/latest?cb=20190915044723)
- **Description:** Fire one more arrow each time
- **Positive Effects:**
  - Additional arrow in every direction you have ability to shoot in
  - Stacks up to three times
- **Side Effects:**
  - -10% base damage
  - -15% attack speed
- **Notes:** Multishot reduces the per arrow damage of all arrows.

### Ricochet
- **Image:** ![Ricochet](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/a/ae/Ricochet-0.png/revision/latest?cb=20190915044836)
- **Description:** Your arrows ricochet between monsters
- **Positive Effects:**
  - Allows your shot to ricochet between enemies and hit 3 times in total
  - Allows star abilities to ricochet between enemies
- **Side Effects:**
  - Reduces damage of the projectile by 30% after each consecutive hit
- **Notes:** Works with other abilities like Holy Effect, Poison, and Blaze. If you also have Pierce, arrows will Ricochet if they can or Pierce if they cannot Ricochet. Triggers Headshots. Extra damage from rings is applied and modified individually on each hit. When using the Tornado, the tornado's damage reduction is used instead.

### Front Arrow +1
- **Image:** ![Front Arrow](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/c/cb/Front_Arrow-0.png/revision/latest?cb=20190915044935)
- **Description:** Arrow fired forward +1
- **Positive Effects:**
  - Shoots an additional front arrow
- **Side Effects:**
  - -25% base damage for ALL arrows (including diagonal, side, etc.)
  - If you have it more than once, you probably will not hit all arrows (especially on small monsters) making total DPS go down on anything but the biggest monsters and bosses
- **Notes:** Front Arrow reduces the per arrow damage of all arrows. This can reduce damage on Staff if you have more than Diagonal Arrows +1.

### Piercing Shot
- **Image:** ![Piercing Shot](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/4/48/Piercing_Shot.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005090620)
- **Description:** Your arrows pierce enemies
- **Effect:** Projectiles pass through multiple enemies
- **Side Effects:**
  - -33% damage for each enemy the projectile goes through
- **Notes:** Reduces damage to Tornado.

### Bouncy Wall
- **Image:** ![Bouncy Wall](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/c/cb/Bouncy_Wall.png/revision/latest?cb=20191007065841)
- **Description:** Arrows bounce against walls
- **Effect:** Your projectiles bounce off walls
- **Side Effects:**
  - -50% damage on each bounce
- **Synergy:**
  - Bad for Tornado
  - Good with Diagonal, Side, Rear, Scythe, Staff

### Diagonal Arrows
- **Image:** ![Diagonal Arrows](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/0/0d/Diagonal_Arrows.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005072508)
- **Description:** Diagonal Arrows +1
- **Effect:** Adds two additional arrows firing diagonally. Can be chosen more than once. Diagonal arrows act as normal arrows with 100% damage and the ability to crit. Additionally diagonal arrows are affected by multishot, blaze, bounce, etc.
- **Notes:** Due to the lack of damage reduction (as opposed to front arrow), stacking multiple diagonal arrows with multishot yields substantial damage boosts, especially with Staff. Synergises well with Bouncy Wall.

### Side Arrows +1
- **Image:** ![Side Arrows](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/a/a3/Side_arrows.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005092341)
- **Description:** Fires an Arrow on your both sides
- **Effect:** Fire additional projectiles to the left and right

### Rear Arrow +1
- **Image:** ![Rear Arrow](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/b/b0/Rear_Arrow.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005090721)
- **Description:** Arrow fired backward +1
- **Effect:** Fire an additional projectile backwards which deals 100% damage
- **Notes:** Works well with Bouncy Wall.

## Elemental Abilities

General elemental info:
- **Lightning (Bolt)** - When an enemy is hit, electricity bolts strike all enemies nearby for 25% of equipment damage (increased by attack boosts). Maximum of one spark per enemy per tick.
- **Freeze** - Enemy freezes for up to 2 sec. Hitting the enemy again unfreezes them but freeze can be applied again after 2s cooldown. Deals 10% modifiable damage each time arrows/stars/meteors/strike swords hit.
- **Poison** - Enemy starts losing HP slowly. Lasts until death. Deals 35% of equipment damage per second. Does not stack.
- **Fire (Blaze)** - Enemy starts burning 0.25s after effect is applied. Lasts 2 seconds. Each tick deals 18% of equipment damage every 0.25s (7 ticks total). Effect is reset every time the enemy is hit.
- Elementals don't cancel each other (e.g. Fire and Ice can coexist).

### Bolt
- **Image:** ![Bolt](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/8/8e/Bolt.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005085457)
- **Description:** Your attacks cause Lightning effect
- **Effect:** Deal 25% equipment damage (increased by attack boosts) to enemies within a few tiles of enemies hit by weapon projectile
- **Notes:** Works well with Ricochet. Maximum of one spark per enemy per tick (doesn't hit twice with Front Arrow or extremely high attack speed).

### Freeze
- **Image:** ![Freeze](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/0/00/Freeze.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005075646)
- **Description:** Slows enemies down
- **Effect:** Freezes enemies for up to 2 sec. Hitting the enemy again unfreezes them but freeze can be applied again after 2s cooldown. Deals 10% modifiable damage each time arrows, stars, meteors, strike swords hit.
- **Notes:** Only freezes enemies; does not slow them. Mobs will be unfrozen by next hit, including pets, multi, etc. Freeze changes behavior of mobs. Some bosses are immune.

### Poison Touch
- **Image:** ![Poison Touch](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/7/7b/Poison_Touch.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005085046)
- **Description:** Your attacks cause Poison effect
- **Effect:** Deals 35% equipment damage per sec until mob dies
- **Notes:** Works well with Ricochet. Does not stack.

### Blaze
- **Image:** ![Blaze](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/b/bd/Blaze.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005084223)
- **Description:** Your attacks cause a Flame effect
- **Effect:** Enemy starts burning 0.25s after effect is applied. The effect lasts 2 seconds. Each tick deals 18% of equipment damage every 0.25s (7 ticks total). The effect is reset every time the enemy is hit.
- **Notes:** Works well with Ricochet. Does not stack.

### Element Upgrade
- **Image:** ![Element Upgrade](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/c/c1/Element_Upgrade.png/revision/latest?cb=20210326154414)
- **Description:** Element damage increased greatly
- **Effect (per element, to be confirmed):**
  - Freeze: increased by 100%
  - Bolt: increased by 150%
  - Poison: increased by 250%
  - Blaze: increased by 100%
  - Dark Touch: increased by 100%

### Element Burst
- **Image:** ![Element Burst](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/8/84/Element_Burst.png/revision/latest?cb=20210326153948)
- **Description:** Various elemental stats, elemental damage has chance for Crit.
- **Effect:** Elemental damage gains a chance to critically hit

### Dark Touch
- **Image:** ![Dark Touch](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/4/44/Dark_Touch.png/revision/latest?cb=20200327043339)
- **Description:** Puts a time bomb on an enemy causing an explosion when it detonates.
- **Effect:** Dark touch places a bomb on a struck target for 1 second. The explosion deals 100% of modified damage from ATK Boosts in an area of effect around the targeted mob.
- **Notes:** Base damage does not affect damage. Multiple darks will not stack on an enemy.

## Strike Abilities (Summoned Swords - Ranged)

**Shared mechanic:** Fires a sword which deals 150% equipment damage (increased by attack boosts) and applies an elemental effect on impact. The amount of attack speed you have can also make more strikes appear faster. A sword is summoned by your side every second an enemy is in range and automatically flies towards your current target. The projectile behaves just like a regular unmodified arrow, colliding with obstacles and enemies. None of this damage can be Critical.

### Blazing Strike
- **Image:** ![Blazing Strike](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/b/b4/Blazing-strike.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005083329)
- **Description:** Summon a Fire Sword to attack enemies
- **Effect:** Each sword hits for 150% of your modified damage (including +Atk and equipment) then applies a fire damage-over-time effect of 15% unmodified hero damage every 0.25 seconds for 2 seconds (7 ticks). Can be applied in addition to other DoT effects.
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 6. Does not spawn if you are too far.

### Frost Strike
- **Image:** ![Frost Strike](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/6/65/Frost_Strike.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005083340)
- **Description:** Summon a Frost Sword to attack enemies
- **Effect:** Each sword hits for 150% of your modified damage (including +Atk and equipment) then temporarily freezes the target (~0.5 second). This effect is only really useful for interrupting enemies, preventing some enemies' current attack or movement.
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 6. Does not spawn if you are too far. Damage cannot be Critical.

### Toxic Strike
- **Image:** ![Toxic Strike](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/c/ce/Toxic-strike.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005073520)
- **Description:** Summon a Toxic Sword to attack enemies
- **Effect:** Each sword hits for 150% of your modified damage (including +Atk and equipment) then applies a permanent poison damage-over-time effect of 30% unmodified hero damage every second. Can be applied in addition to other DoT effects.
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 6. Does not spawn if you are too far. Damage cannot be Critical.

### Bolt Strike
- **Image:** ![Bolt Strike](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/5/5e/Bolt_Strike.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005083350)
- **Description:** Summons a Bolt Sword to attack enemies
- **Effect:** Each sword hits for 150% of your modified damage (including +Atk and equipment) then shocks other enemies near the target for 25% of your modified damage.
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 6. Does not spawn if you are too far. Damage cannot be Critical.

## Health-Dependent Abilities

### Fury
- **Image:** ![Fury](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/4/46/Fury.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005083432)
- **Description:** Your attacks faster at low HP
- **Effect:** 0.4% attack speed per 1% missing health
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 7. Helix hero has an enhanced version called Fury (Helix) which functions as Rage but at 1.2% additional damage per % of lost health (20% stronger than Rage).

### Rage
- **Image:** ![Rage](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/2/27/Rage.png/revision/latest?cb=20191005090334)
- **Description:** Your attacks deal more damage at low HP
- **Effect:** Increases the damage of your arrows proportionally to the percent of your missing health (multiplicative). e.g. 10 health remaining out of 1000 maximum health increases your damage by 99%.
- **Notes:** Top tier skill. Helix cannot roll this ability because he has built-in Fury (Helix).

### Grace
- **Image:** ![Grace](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/1/17/Grace.png/revision/latest?cb=20200423071744)
- **Description:** You restore more HP at low HP
- **Effect:** For every 1% of HP missing, Red hearts will heal for +0.6% more HP.
- **Notes:** Health-based ability. Can be obtained from leveling up and Lucky Wheel. Unlocks in Chapter 7.

### Agility
- **Image:** ![Agility](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/archero/images/1/14/Agility.png/revision/latest?cb=20191007062917)
- **Description:** Lower the HP, easier to dodge enemy attacks
- **Effect:** 0.3% dodge per 1% missing health
- **Notes:** Unlocks in Chapter 7.

- **Notes:** Dodge chance is multiplicative, not additive. Formula: `1 - ((1 - dodge1) * (1 - dodge2) * ...)` where each dodge source is its chance to hit.
